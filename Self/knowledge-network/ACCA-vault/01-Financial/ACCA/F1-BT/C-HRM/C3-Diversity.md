# C3 — Diversity & Inclusion

---

## 🎯 Diversity vs Inclusion

> **Diversity** = Being invited to the party  
> **Inclusion** = Being asked to dance

| Concept | Definition | Focus |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Diversity** | Team has people from different backgrounds | Representation / Numbers |
| **Inclusion** | Everyone's voice is heard and respected | Participation / Belonging |
| **Belonging** | Can be your authentic self | Psychological safety |

---

## 🌈 Dimensions of Diversity

| Dimension | Examples |
|:---|:---|
| **Age** | Multi-generational (Gen Z / Millennial / Gen X / Boomer) |
| **Gender** | Gender balance, equal pay |
| **Ethnicity** | Racial/ethnic diversity |
| **Disability** | Accessible workplace |
| **Sexual Orientation** | LGBT+ inclusion |
| **Cognitive** | Diversity of thought (most important, most overlooked) |

---

## 💼 Business Case for Diversity

| Advantage | Mechanism |
|:---|:---|
| **Innovation** | Different perspectives generate more ideas |
| **Decision Quality** | Reduces Groupthink |
| **Talent Attraction** | Inclusive culture attracts wider talent pool |
| **Market Understanding** | Diverse teams better understand diverse customers |
| **Employer Brand** | Enhanced corporate image |

⚠️ **Debate**: Is the business case sufficient? If diversity doesn't increase profit in a given scenario, is it no longer needed?

---

## 🔍 Unconscious Bias

| Bias Type | Manifestation |
|:---|:---|
| **Affinity Bias** | Tendency to favour people similar to oneself |
| **Gender Bias** | Default assumption that certain roles belong to certain genders |
| **Name Bias** | Preference for certain names (studies show same CV with different names gets different response rates) |
| **Beauty Bias** | Higher evaluation of physically attractive people |

---

## ⚖️ Quota vs Target

|  | Quota | Target |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Nature** | Mandatory, must achieve | Aspirational, encouraged |
| **Risk** | Potential "reverse discrimination" | May lack enforcement |
| **Legal** | Accepted in some jurisdictions | Widely acceptable |

## 🏠 Virtual Workplace & Diversity

Impact of remote/hybrid work on diversity:
- ✅ Expands geographic talent pool → more diverse
- ✅ Flexible work attracts different needs (disabled, carers)
- ❌ Reduces informal interaction → inclusion may decrease
- ❌ Digital divide → new inequalities

# 商业英语场景模板

## 创业路演 (Startup Pitch)

### 基础路演模板
```
Opening: Hello, I'm [name] from [company]. We're solving [problem] for [target market].

Problem: [Define the problem clearly and why it matters]

Solution: Our product/service is [brief description], which [key benefits].

Market Size: Our target market is [size] and growing at [rate] annually.

Business Model: We make money by [revenue model]. Our pricing is [pricing structure].

Competition: Our main competitors are [list], but we differentiate by [unique advantage].

Traction: So far, we've achieved [key metrics/milestones].

Team: Our team brings expertise in [relevant experience/skills].

Ask: We're raising [amount] to [key objectives]. This will get us to [next milestone].

Closing: Thank you for your time. I'm happy to answer any questions.
```

### 高级路演要点
- 开场需抓人眼球 (hook)
- 问题陈述要有共鸣和紧迫感
- 解决方案要简明易懂
- 使用具体数字而非泛泛而谈
- 展示清晰的盈利路径
- 强调团队优势和执行能力
- 投资用途明确具体

## 投资人会面 (Investor Meeting)

### 典型问题与回答框架

#### 产品/服务问题
```
Q: How exactly does your product/service work?
A: [简单解释 → 用户体验 → 技术优势 → 实际案例]

Q: What's your IP or competitive moat?
A: [专利/技术 → 网络效应 → 先发优势 → 难以复制的资源]
```

#### 市场问题
```
Q: Why now? What's changed in the market?
A: [市场趋势 → 技术变革 → 监管变化 → 用户行为转变]

Q: Who are your biggest competitors and how do you differentiate?
A: [直接竞争对手 → 间接替代品 → 差异点 → 为何重要]
```

#### 财务问题
```
Q: What are your unit economics?
A: [CAC → LTV → 毛利率 → 回收周期 → 规模效应]

Q: How will you use the funds and what milestones will you hit?
A: [资金分配 → 关键指标 → 时间线 → 下一轮条件]
```

#### 团队问题
```
Q: Why is your team uniquely positioned to solve this problem?
A: [相关经验 → 互补技能 → 领域知识 → 过往成就]

Q: What keeps you up at night? What are your biggest risks?
A: [诚实面对风险 → 缓解策略 → 学习能力 → 适应计划]
```

## 商务谈判 (Business Negotiation)

### 谈判框架
```
Opening: [建立关系 → 确认议程 → 设定期望]

Interest Exploration: [了解对方需求 → 解释自己需求 → 寻找共同点]

Value Creation: [提出方案 → 解释优势 → 处理异议]

Bargaining: [初始提议 → 让步空间 → 价值交换]

Agreement: [总结条款 → 确认下一步 → 建立长期关系]
```

### 常用谈判表达
- "I understand your position on X. From our perspective..."
- "What if we could find a way to address your concern about Y while still..."
- "Perhaps we could compromise on X if you could be flexible on Y..."
- "Let me make sure I understand correctly. You're saying that..."
- "We've come a long way. Let's see if we can bridge this final gap..."

## 投资者演示 (Investor Demo)

### 演示结构
```
Problem Setup: [建立情境 → 展示痛点]

Product Demo: [核心功能 → 用户体验 → 差异点]

Metrics & Impact: [关键数据 → 用户反馈 → 商业影响]

Roadmap: [近期功能 → 长期愿景]

Q&A Preparation: [技术细节 → 商业问题 → 扩展计划]
```

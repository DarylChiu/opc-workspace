🔧 Grammar & Phrasing to Improve

You said I will play guitar and and watch movie → I play guitar, watch movies — Remove 'will' for habitual actions; avoid double 'and' and use plural 'movies'.
You said So fucking surprised → I was absolutely amazed — Avoid informal/swear words in IELTS; use more formal intensifiers.
You said I cannot get a systematic lesson at the beginning → I didn't receive formal lessons at the start — 'Cannot' is present tense; past tense needed here.
You said the express view of that city → the impressive skyline of that city — 'Express view' is unclear; 'skyline' is more precise.

⭐ Strong Points & Better Expressions

Good use of I have been playing guitar for 10 years → **I have been playing guitar for a decade** — 'A decade' sounds more sophisticated and precise.
Good use of I spent almost years in Chengdu and make some truly friends → **I spent several years in Chengdu and made some genuine friends** — 'Genuine' is a stronger adjective than 'truly'.
Good use of I enjoyed the gathering with the friends in Chengdu → **I enjoyed reuniting with my friends in Chengdu** — 'Reuniting' implies a special occasion and is more vivid.

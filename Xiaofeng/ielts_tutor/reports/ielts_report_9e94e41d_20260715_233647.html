⭐ Strong Points & Better Expressions

Good use of there are so many kinds of assignments projects that I'm handling → **I'm handling a wide variety of assignments and projects** — 'a wide variety' is more formal and precise.
Good use of it's kind of easy for me to just schedule for the homework → **I find it quite easy to schedule my homework** — 'find it quite easy' is more natural and avoids the filler 'kind of'.
Good use of I'm Starting Wehiko like car or maybe maybe the Something like that → **I'm starting with Wehiko, like cars or something similar** — 'starting with' clarifies the focus, and 'something similar' is more concise.
